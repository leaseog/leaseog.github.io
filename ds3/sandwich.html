In the Kitchen..


On a quiet Saturday evening, Lea turned on the gas stove and placed a pan on the burner. She cracked an egg with a quick tap on the bowl and poured it into the pan, the sizzle rising as it hit the heat. After covering the pan with a soft clink, she grabbed some bread and started preparing for a sandwich. While the egg cooked, she snacked on something crunchy, each bite crisp in the quiet room. When the cooking bell rang, she slid the egg onto the bread, added some toppings, and finished her sandwich. Sitting down with her meal, she poured herself a cold beer, the fizz rising in the glass, and took a sip as she enjoyed her freshly made sandwich.

A moment later... she had sandwich too much, so she got indigestion. Now the ear acupuncture is needed.
